Our Story

Raah Screen Print is a printing company which was established in 1994 AD. It has been effectively running for last 28 years. Raah Screen Print is located in Teku, Kathmandu. It is a small scale business run by small group of people. We provide different kind of products.we produces good-quality prints at affordable prices.We provide all kinds of printing products which can be served as gifts and souvenirs on any occasion.We serve products like cups, multicolor t-shirts, masks, cushions, bags, bottles, mousepads, digital t-shirts, etc. It is a business run by a small family with 100% authentic and guaranteed prints.
